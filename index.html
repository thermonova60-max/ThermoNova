How It Works

The Peltier Effect

When electric current flows through a thermoelectric module, heat transfers from one side to the other — creating a cold and hot surface instantly. No compressors, no moving parts, and no harmful gases.

Cooling Cycle

  • DC power supplied to TEC module
  • Cold side absorbs heat → cooling chamber
  • Hot side dissipates heat using heatsink + fan
  • Temperature controlled via microcontroller
